INFO

Experience the adrenaline-fueled thrill of Castle Cruisers, a fast-paced sci-fi racing game set in a cosmic castle. Pilot your space racer through treacherous tracks, firing lasers at opponents and collecting power-ups for an edge. Ignite your engines, defy gravity, and become the ultimate champion!

ABOUT

Castle Cruisers was the resulting game when the objective was to create a game based on drawing cards. Our team drew Racing, Sci-fi and Castle (or something along those lines I honestly can't remember). 4 weeks was not enough time for everything we had planned, predictably. My role was to create the controller for the car, making a hover vehicle meant I had to program all the physics for it from scratch. It was a fun little project that is sadly lacking a castle.

PLAY

To exit the game you can only use ALT+F4 or end the task in task manager

Controls are W, A, S and D to control the vehicle
Space to brake
Left Mouse Button to fire a laser

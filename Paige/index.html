INFO

Join Paige, a courageous girl on an extraordinary quest to save her beloved Teddy bear. Shrunken to miniature size, armed with a humble thimble as her weapon, she battles through hordes of rats. Traverse enchanting landscapes, uncover hidden secrets, and face off against the malevolent Rat King. Will you help Paige defeat her adversaries and reunite with Teddy? Prepare for a heartwarming adventure like no other!

ABOUT

The final game from my first year at university, we had a fairly sizeable team on paper, but this project was mostly the work of myself, another programmer, a designer and an artist. With unfulfilled promises of work from various other members, Paige ended up being an extremely short experience missing tons of features from other team members who didn't show up.

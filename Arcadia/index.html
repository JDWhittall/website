INFO

Unearth the secrets of a mysterious temple in Arcadia, a captivating 2D adventure game. Dive into the role of an archaeologist, exploring ancient artifacts and solving puzzles. Immerse yourself in an enchanting world of discovery and unlock the temple's hidden treasures. Will you unravel its ancient mysteries? Begin your archaeological journey in Arcadia today!

ABOUT

The first university project. I have fond memories of working on this project for the 2 weeks we had to complete it. With a fairly large team all of which were involved in it's creation (Not a theme that continued through my academic career). I created a custom character controller for this one, and soon realised that moving platforms don't "just work".

A terrifyingly simple 2d platformer with large walls of text for all the story and lore the writers made, it reminds me of a simpler time in gaming.

PLAY

To exit the game you can only use ALT+F4 or end the task in task manager

Controls are A and D to move left and right
Space to jump
E to interact with an object
F to stop interacting with an object

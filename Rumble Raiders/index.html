INFO

Rumble Raiders is a pacman-esque multiplayer game - race against other players to collect the most loot and get the highest score, setting traps and stealing their points as you go. Avoid the enemies prowling the corridors as they try to hunt down what's rightfully theirs!

Play with up to 4 players online, setting boobie traps and using your abilities against them to steal their loot and raise your own score.

Get in and get out! The timer is ticking, and the pressure is on - when the clock strikes, Doctor Jekyll changes and Mr Hyde comes out to play...

Loot the place while setting and using traps against the enemy players - how will you play? Sneak around with strategic bear-traps? Or lure Hyde into their path? It's your choice!
